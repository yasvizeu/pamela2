Dialogue

Marina & Oliver

Marina is Brazilian and Oliver is British. They are at the airport. Marina is tired because she is traveling for work, but she is happy to practice English.

Oliver is drinking coffee and waiting for his flight. They are talking about their routines and their jobs.

Oliver: Hi. Are you waiting for your flight too?
Marina: Yes, I am. I am tired today.
Oliver: Do you travel for work?
Marina: Yes, I do. I work a lot.
Oliver: Does your job stress you?
Marina: Sometimes. But I like my job.
Oliver: I drink coffee every morning. Do you drink coffee too?
Marina: Yes, I do. I love Brazilian coffee.
Oliver: What is your daily routine like?
Marina: I wake up early, brush my teeth, take a shower and go to work.
